## Directory Roles

### `/docs`
High-level explanatory material, maps, and orientation documents.
No code execution expected.

### `/theory`
Formal theoretical definitions:
- Unified Lagrangian
- Field equations
- Collapse and measurement models
- Glossaries and symbol definitions

### `/code`
Executable research code:
- simulations
- Bayesian inference
- numerical experiments
- reproducibility scripts

### `/data`
Raw and processed experimental constraints and datasets
used by simulations and inference code.

### `/experiments`
Proposed and exploratory tests of the framework.
These may evolve or be superseded.

### `/papers`
Manuscripts in LaTeX, Markdown, and PDF form,
including published, submitted, and draft works.

### `/archive`
Historical or superseded material retained for provenance
and scholarly traceability.
